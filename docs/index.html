FAQ

Where is my data stored?▾
Entries are saved on your device by default. If you enable iCloud, they’re stored in your private iCloud database tied to your Apple ID.
Can you access my entries?▾
No. Havn has no accounts or servers. If you turn on iCloud, Apple hosts the data in your private CloudKit container; the developer cannot see it.
How does iCloud sync work?▾
When enabled, changes sync through CloudKit between your devices signed into the same Apple ID with iCloud Drive on. Open Havn on both devices to nudge a sync.
How do I restore on a new iPhone?▾
Sign in with the same Apple ID, enable iCloud Drive, install Havn, then open it. Your entries will appear as CloudKit sync completes.
Why one entry per day?▾
Focus and reflection. One photo + note keeps the habit light, consistent, and easier to look back on without noise.
How do I lock the app?▾
Go to Settings → App Lock to enable Face ID / Touch ID. Biometric checks are handled by iOS; Havn never sees your biometrics.
Do you collect analytics?▾
No tracking and no third‑party analytics. We rely on user emails for feedback and Apple’s crash reports for stability.
How can I contact support?▾
Email zac@seebeck.work with your iPhone model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce any issue.
