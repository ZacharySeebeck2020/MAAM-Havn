Sync tips

  • Use the same Apple ID on all devices with iCloud Drive enabled.
  • Enable Settings → iCloud Sync in Havn.
  • Open Havn on both devices to nudge a sync. Low Power Mode can delay background sync.

Privacy basics

  • Entries are stored on your device unless you enable iCloud.
  • iCloud uses your private CloudKit database; the developer cannot access it.
  • App Lock uses Face ID/Touch ID via iOS; biometrics never leave your device.
